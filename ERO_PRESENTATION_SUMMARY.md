## 🎯 **PLATFORM OVERVIEW**

### **Core Mission**

TeAoMarama is a comprehensive educational resources platform designed specifically for New Zealand teachers and students, with deep cultural integration and curriculum alignment.

- **5,439+ Educational Resources** across 8 subject areas
- **3,372 Cultural Resources** with authentic Māori integration
- **370 High Priority Resources** for immediate classroom use
- **Professional Design** with modern, responsive interface
- **Performance Optimized** for fast loading and accessibility
