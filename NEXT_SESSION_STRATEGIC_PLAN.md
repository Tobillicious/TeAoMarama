## 🌊 CULTURAL RESPONSIBILITY FRAMEWORK

### Every Technical Decision Evaluated Through:

#### **Manaakitanga** - Care for Users
- Does this change enhance user well-being?
- Will this improve the learning experience?
- Does this respect user dignity and autonomy?

#### **Whakatōhea** - Collective Responsibility  
- How does this serve the broader community?
- Will this strengthen iwi relationships?
- Does this contribute to collective educational success?

#### **Kaitiakitanga** - Guardian Stewardship
- Are we protecting cultural knowledge appropriately?
- Is this sustainable for future generations?
- Does this honor our responsibility as digital guardians?

#### **Whakapapa** - Relationship Understanding
- How does this connect to existing systems and knowledge?
- What are the relationship implications of this change?
- Does this strengthen or weaken important connections?
