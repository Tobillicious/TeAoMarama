## Cultural Authenticity Protocol

### Te Ao Māori Design Principles:
1. **Whakapapa** (Relationships): Design reflects connections and relationships
2. **Manaakitanga** (Hospitality): Welcoming, accessible interface
3. **Rangatiratanga** (Leadership): Professional excellence with cultural pride
4. **Kotahitanga** (Unity): Harmonious design elements working together

### Color Cultural Significance:
- **Teal/Turquoise**: Connection to moana (ocean), life force
- **Deep Greens**: Connection to whenua (land), growth
- **Warm Earth Tones**: Connection to ancestry, tradition
- **Gold Accents**: Excellence, achievement, kōwhai flower
