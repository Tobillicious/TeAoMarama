Honouring Te Ao Māori

This lesson template is designed to honour the principles of culturally responsive pedagogy. Consider how your lesson:

  • Affirms cultural identity - How does the lesson validate and celebrate diverse cultural backgrounds?
  • Connects to whakapapa - How does learning connect to students' genealogy of knowledge?
  • Builds cultural competence - How does the lesson develop understanding across cultures?
  • Supports academic achievement - How do cultural connections enhance rather than compromise academic rigor?
  • Develops critical consciousness - How does the lesson help students understand power, privilege, and social justice?
