## 🌺 Cultural Context

Kaitiakitanga and the health of our local awa, the Mangakōtukutuku River.

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

1. Understand and apply concepts of rate of change to measure water flow.
2. Collect, represent, and analyze water quality data (e.g., pH, clarity) using statistical measures.
3. Use geometric principles to map the river's course and identify significant locations.
4. Connect mathematical findings to the principle of kaitiakitanga and the stories of Ngāti Kahungunu.
