# Product Guide

## Te Kura o TeAoMarama - The School of the World of Light

Always maintain cultural sensitivity and understanding when working with this educational platform for Aotearoa New Zealand.

## Product Purpose

Te Kura o TeAoMarama is New Zealand's premier AI-enhanced educational platform that provides:
- Culturally responsive educational resources aligned with NZ curriculum
- Māori cultural contexts integrated into all learning materials
- AI-powered educational intelligence through Mihara (Kaitiaki Mahara - Guardian of Memory)
- Multi-level educational content for Years 1-13
- Assessment frameworks and tools for New Zealand educators

## Core Features

### Educational Resources
- Subject-specific lessons (English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies)
- Year-level appropriate content (Y1-Y13)
- Cultural integration with Te Reo Māori and tikanga Māori
- Assessment templates and rubrics
- Interactive learning activities

### AI Enhancement
- Mihara superintelligence system for content optimization
- Multi-agent coordination for educational excellence
- Real-time platform monitoring and adaptation
- Cultural safety protocol enforcement

### Platform Capabilities
- Resource discovery and filtering
- Educational dashboard with progress tracking
- Responsive design for all devices
- Performance optimization for New Zealand schools

## Key Business Rules

### Cultural Safety Protocol
- All content must respect Māori cultural values and tikanga
- Te Reo Māori integration is mandatory where appropriate
- Cultural consultation protocols must be followed
- No content should undermine indigenous knowledge systems

### Educational Standards
- All resources must align with New Zealand Curriculum Framework
- Content must meet ERO (Education Review Office) standards
- Assessment tools must be valid and reliable
- Learning outcomes must be clearly defined

### Technical Requirements
- Platform must be accessible and performant
- Content must be searchable and discoverable
- All educational resources must be validated before deployment
- System must support concurrent users across New Zealand schools

## User Value Proposition

For **Educators**: Provides ready-to-use, culturally appropriate educational resources that save preparation time while ensuring curriculum alignment.

For **Students**: Offers engaging, culturally relevant learning experiences that connect to their identity and heritage while meeting educational standards.

For **Administrators**: Delivers measurable educational outcomes with comprehensive assessment frameworks and progress tracking.

## Success Metrics
- Resource usage across New Zealand schools
- Cultural appropriateness ratings
- Educational outcome improvements
- Teacher satisfaction and time savings
- Student engagement metrics
