# 🤝 **Community Partnership Framework**

## **Mangakōtukutuku College - Local Engagement & Collaboration**

---

### **🎯 PARTNERSHIP OBJECTIVE**

Establish **strong, sustainable partnerships** with local community organizations, marae, cultural advisors, and families to create the world's best culturally-integrated digital education platform.

---

## **🏛️ PARTNERSHIP STRUCTURE**

### **TIER 1: CORE CULTURAL PARTNERS**

- **Local Marae** - Cultural protocols and traditional knowledge
- **Ngāti Kahungunu Representatives** - Iwi leadership and guidance
- **Cultural Advisors** - Expert knowledge and validation
- **Kaumātua** - Traditional wisdom and community leadership

### **TIER 2: EDUCATIONAL PARTNERS**

- **Local Schools** - Cross-institutional collaboration
- **Educational Organizations** - Best practices and resources
- **Teacher Networks** - Professional development and sharing
- **Student Organizations** - Youth leadership and engagement

### **TIER 3: COMMUNITY ORGANIZATIONS**

- **Environmental Groups** - Kaitiakitanga and sustainability
- **Cultural Organizations** - Arts, language, and traditions
- **Family Support Services** - Parent engagement and support
- **Youth Development Groups** - Student leadership and growth

---

## **🌿 MARAE PARTNERSHIPS**

### **PARTNERSHIP OBJECTIVES:**

- **Cultural protocol** guidance and validation
- **Traditional knowledge** sharing and protection
- **Community events** and celebrations
- **Student cultural** immersion experiences

### **COLLABORATION ACTIVITIES:**

1. **Cultural protocol** training for teachers
2. **Traditional knowledge** integration in lessons
3. **Marae visits** and cultural experiences
4. **Community celebrations** and ceremonies
5. **Cultural advisor** training and support

### **EXPECTED OUTCOMES:**

- **Authentic cultural** content for platform
- **Student cultural** competency development
- **Community trust** and engagement
- **Traditional knowledge** preservation

---

## **👥 CULTURAL ADVISOR NETWORK**

### **ADVISOR ROLES:**

- **Content validation** and cultural safety
- **Protocol guidance** for teachers and students
- **Traditional knowledge** sharing and protection
- **Community connection** and relationship building

### **SELECTION CRITERIA:**

- **Deep knowledge** of Ngāti Kahungunu traditions
- **Community respect** and recognition
- **Educational experience** or interest
- **Commitment** to cultural preservation
- **Availability** for regular consultation

### **TRAINING AND SUPPORT:**

- **Platform orientation** and training
- **Cultural safety** protocol training
- **Content validation** process training
- **Community engagement** strategy support
