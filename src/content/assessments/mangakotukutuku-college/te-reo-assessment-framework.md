# 📊 **Te Reo Māori Assessment Framework**

## **Mangakōtukutuku College - Year 9-10**

---

### **🎯 ASSESSMENT OVERVIEW**

This framework provides **immediately implementable** assessment tools for Te Reo Māori learning at Mangakōtukutuku College, focusing on local dialect, cultural competency, and community engagement.

---

## **📋 ASSESSMENT STRUCTURE**

### **FORMATIVE ASSESSMENTS (Ongoing)**

- **Weekly vocabulary quizzes** (10 minutes)
- **Cultural protocol observations** (during activities)
- **Community engagement tracking** (participation logs)
- **Dialect practice recordings** (audio submissions)

### **SUMMATIVE ASSESSMENTS (End of Term)**

- **Oral presentation** (15 minutes)
- **Written reflection** (500 words)
- **Community project** (ongoing)
- **Cultural competency demonstration** (practical)

---

## **🎭 ASSESSMENT TASKS**

### **TASK 1: Local Dialect Oral Presentation**

#### **Duration:** 15 minutes

#### **Weight:** 30%

#### **Description:**

Students present a 3-5 minute speech in Ngāti Kahungunu dialect about a local topic of their choice.

#### **Assessment Criteria:**

- **Pronunciation accuracy** (25%)
- **Vocabulary usage** (25%)
- **Cultural context** (25%)
- **Community relevance** (25%)

#### **Rubric:**

| **Excellence (A)**                                                 | **Merit (B)**                                             | **Achievement (C)**                                            | **Not Achieved (D)**                                              |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Fluent local dialect, rich vocabulary, deep cultural understanding | Good local dialect, varied vocabulary, cultural awareness | Basic local dialect, limited vocabulary, some cultural context | Minimal dialect usage, basic vocabulary, limited cultural context |

---

### **TASK 2: Cultural Reflection Essay**

#### **Duration:** 2 weeks

#### **Weight:** 25%

#### **Description:**

Students write a 500-word reflection on their cultural learning journey and community connections.

#### **Assessment Criteria:**

- **Personal reflection depth** (30%)
- **Cultural understanding** (30%)
- **Community connections** (25%)
- **Language integration** (15%)

#### **Writing Prompts:**

1. "How has learning the local dialect changed your understanding of our community?"
2. "What cultural practices have you observed and how do they connect to your learning?"
3. "How can you apply this knowledge to strengthen community relationships?"

---

### **TASK 3: Community Engagement Project**

#### **Duration:** 6 weeks

#### **Weight:** 35%

#### **Description:**

Students design and implement a project that connects their Te Reo learning with community engagement.

#### **Project Options:**

- **Marae visit and documentation** (with family permission)
- **Local story collection** from community members
- **Cultural event participation** and reflection
- **Environmental stewardship** project using local knowledge

#### **Assessment Criteria:**

- **Project planning** (20%)
- **Community engagement** (30%)
- **Cultural sensitivity** (25%)
- **Reflection and learning** (25%)

---

### **TASK 4: Cultural Competency Demonstration**

#### **Duration:** 1 week

#### **Weight:** 10%

#### **Description:**

Students demonstrate their understanding of local cultural protocols and practices.

#### **Demonstration Options:**

- **Marae etiquette** role-play
- **Cultural greeting** and introduction
- **Traditional practice** explanation
- **Community protocol** demonstration

---

## **📊 MARKING SCHEMES**

### **OVERALL GRADE CALCULATION:**

- **A (Excellence):** 85-100%
- **B (Merit):** 70-84%
- **C (Achievement):** 50-69%
- **D (Not Achieved):** 0-49%

### **GRADE DESCRIPTORS:**

#### **A (Excellence):**

- **Demonstrates** fluent local dialect usage
- **Shows** deep cultural understanding and respect
- **Actively engages** with community and cultural practices
- **Integrates** language and culture seamlessly
- **Exceeds** expected learning outcomes

#### **B (Merit):**

- **Demonstrates** good local dialect usage
- **Shows** solid cultural understanding
- **Engages** with community practices
- **Connects** language and culture effectively
- **Meets** all learning outcomes

#### **C (Achievement):**

- **Demonstrates** basic local dialect usage
- **Shows** some cultural understanding
- **Participates** in community practices
- **Begins to connect** language and culture
- **Meets** most learning outcomes

#### **D (Not Achieved):**

- **Demonstrates** minimal dialect usage
- **Shows** limited cultural understanding
- **Limited participation** in community practices
- **Struggles to connect** language and culture
- **Does not meet** learning outcomes
