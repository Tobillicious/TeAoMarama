## 💰 OPTIMIZED PRICING STRATEGY

### 1. **Free Explorer Tier** (Acquisition-Focused)
- **Price**: $0
- **Purpose**: Lead generation and product demonstration
- **Features**: 5 verified lesson plans, Te Tiriti sample, basic library
- **Conversion Goal**: 15% to paid plans within 30 days

### 2. **Teacher Pro** (Individual Teachers)
- **Monthly**: $25 NZD (was $29) 
- **Yearly**: $250 NZD (was $290) - 17% savings
- **Target**: Individual teachers and ECE educators
- **Value Prop**: Complete authentic NZ curriculum resources

### 3. **School & Kura** (Institutional)
- **Monthly**: $149 NZD (was $199)
- **Yearly**: $1,490 NZD (was $1,990) - 17% savings  
- **Target**: Schools, kāhui ako, education providers
- **Value Prop**: Whole-school transformation with cultural advisory
