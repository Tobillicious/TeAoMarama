## 🎯 **DEMO SCENARIOS**

### **Scenario 1: Mathematics Teacher**

**Challenge**: "I need engaging algebra lessons that connect to Māori culture"

**Demo Flow**:

1. Navigate to Mathematics resources
2. Show "Algebra and Patterns: Māori Mathematical Thinking"
3. Highlight Te Reo Māori terms (tātai, raupapa, whakawhiti)
4. Demonstrate whakairo pattern activities
5. Show assessment rubrics and external resources

**Teacher Reaction**: _"This is exactly what I need! The cultural integration is perfect!"_

### **Scenario 2: Science Teacher**

**Challenge**: "How can I teach environmental science with authentic Māori perspectives?"

**Demo Flow**:

1. Access Science resources
2. Show "Environmental Science: Māori Perspectives on Sustainability"
3. Highlight kaitiakitanga (guardianship) activities
4. Demonstrate connection to local environmental projects
5. Show verified external links to DOC and Te Papa

**Teacher Reaction**: _"The environmental focus with Māori knowledge is brilliant!"_

### **Scenario 3: English Teacher**

**Challenge**: "I want to integrate Te Reo Māori into creative writing"

**Demo Flow**:

1. Navigate to English resources
2. Show "Creative Writing: Integrating Te Reo Māori"
3. Highlight pūrākau (traditional stories) analysis
4. Demonstrate cultural connection activities
5. Show assessment criteria and examples

**Teacher Reaction**: _"This makes Te Reo integration so natural and meaningful!"_

### **Scenario 4: New Teacher**

**Challenge**: "I'm overwhelmed with planning and need ready-to-use resources"

**Demo Flow**:

1. Show comprehensive resource library
2. Demonstrate search and filter capabilities
3. Highlight complete lesson plans with all materials
4. Show assessment tools and rubrics
5. Demonstrate cultural safety guidelines

**Teacher Reaction**: _"This will save me hours of planning time!"_
