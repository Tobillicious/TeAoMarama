### **🎯 ONBOARDING OBJECTIVE**

Transform teachers from **demo users** to **confident platform implementers** within 2 hours, enabling immediate use of Te Kura o TeAoMarama for real classroom instruction.

---

## **🚀 QUICK START (30 MINUTES)**

### **STEP 1: Platform Access (5 minutes)**

1. **Navigate to:** `teao-marama.web.app`
2. **Click:** "Sign In" button
3. **Use:** Your school email address
4. **Password:** [Will be provided by admin]
5. **Role:** Select "Teacher" or "Kaiako"

### **STEP 2: Profile Setup (10 minutes)**

1. **Complete** your teacher profile
2. **Select** your subject areas
3. **Choose** your year levels
4. **Upload** a profile photo (optional)
5. **Set** your cultural competency level

### **STEP 3: First Lesson Creation (15 minutes)**

1. **Navigate to:** "Quality Lessons" section
2. **Click:** "Create New Lesson"
3. **Use** the lesson template provided
4. **Customize** for your class needs
5. **Save** and preview your lesson
