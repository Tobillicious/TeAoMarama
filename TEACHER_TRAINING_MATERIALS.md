## 🌿 **TRAINING MODULE 4: CULTURAL INTEGRATION**

### **Understanding Māori Cultural Elements**

#### **Key Cultural Concepts**:

- **Whakapapa**: Family connections and genealogy
- **Mana**: Authority, respect, and cultural protocols
- **Kaitiakitanga**: Guardianship and environmental responsibility
- **Tikanga**: Cultural protocols and practices

#### **Te Reo Māori Integration**:

##### **Mathematics Terms**:

- **tātai** - to calculate
- **raupapa** - sequence/pattern
- **whakawhiti** - to solve
- **tatau** - to count
- **tau** - number
- **tauranga** - equation

##### **Environmental Terms**:

- **kaitiakitanga** - guardianship
- **taiao** - environment
- **mātauranga taiao** - environmental knowledge
- **whenua** - land
- **moana** - sea/ocean

##### **Language Terms**:

- **pūrākau** - traditional story
- **kōrero** - to speak/tell
- **mana** - authority/power
- **whānau** - family

### **Cultural Safety Guidelines**:

1. **Respect**: Always approach Māori culture with respect
2. **Authenticity**: Use authentic resources and terminology
3. **Community**: Connect with local iwi and cultural experts
4. **Learning**: Continue to learn and grow in cultural understanding

### **Hands-On Practice**:

#### **Activity 1: Exploring Cultural Elements**

3. Review Te Reo Māori terms
4. Understand cultural activities

#### **Activity 2: Planning Cultural Integration**
