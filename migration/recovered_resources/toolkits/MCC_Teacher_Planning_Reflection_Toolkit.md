# Mangakotukutuku College – Teacher Planning & Reflection Toolkit\n\nPurpose: Reduce planning/admin time so kaiako can focus on building strong relationships with ākonga. These templates are lightweight, culturally safe, and aligned with NZC/NCEA.\n\nHow to use\n- Copy any template from ./templates into your planning folder each term/week.\n- Keep cultural safety at the forefront: complete the Cultural Safety Checklist before publishing materials.\n- Log whānau engagement and reflections weekly; small habits compound.\n\nIncluded templates\n- Weekly Planner\n- Lesson Plan Template\n- Unit Plan Template\n- Reflective Practice Log\n- Whānau Engagement Log\n- Cultural Safety Checklist\n- NCEA Progress Tracker (CSV)\n- Student Profile Snapshot\n\nKaitiaki notes\n- Any content containing Māori contexts or local iwi references must be flagged for Kaitiaki review.\n- If unsure, mark for review and proceed with care (he waka eke noa).\n
