# Formative Assessment Checklist (Teacher Use)

CULTURAL FLAG: When using cultural contexts/examples, confirm appropriateness with local guidance before classroom use.

## Observable Behaviours (Tick/Comment)
- [ ] Students explain their strategy using correct vocabulary
- [ ] Students use multiple representations (diagram, table, number line)
- [ ] Students justify why a method works (reasoning, not just answer)
- [ ] Students peer-check respectfully and provide specific feedback
- [ ] Students connect learning to prior knowledge or local context (CULTURAL FLAG)
- [ ] Students self-assess with evidence (not just a number)
- [ ] Students ask clarifying questions about meaning, not only procedures

## Evidence to Collect
- Student work samples (annotated)
- Exit tickets and mini-whiteboard snapshots
- Audio/notes from student explanations
- Misconceptions list and next-steps plan

## Next Steps Planning
- Re-teach to small group on ________
- Adjust task complexity for ________
- Introduce scaffold (sentence stems, worked examples)
- Plan cultural/local examples with whānau/community (CULTURAL FLAG)
