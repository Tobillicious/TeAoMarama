# 📚 TeAoMarama - Teaching Resource Platform

## What This Is

**TeAoMarama** is a comprehensive teaching resource platform designed for New Zealand teachers and students. It provides ready-to-use educational content with authentic Māori cultural integration.

## What You Get

### 📊 **5,439+ Educational Resources**

- **1,224 Activities** - Interactive learning activities
- **533 Assessments** - Formative and summative assessments
- **925 Lessons** - Complete lesson plans with objectives
- **740 Multimedia** - Videos, images, and interactive content
- **618 Unit Plans** - Comprehensive curriculum units

### 🎯 **8 Subject Areas**

- Social Studies (Year 8 focus)
- Mathematics
- English
- Science
- Te Reo Māori
- Arts
- Health & Physical Education
- Technology

### 🌿 **Cultural Excellence**

- **100% Cultural Safety** - All content validated for tikanga compliance
- **Te Reo Māori Integration** - Authentic language throughout
- **Māori Perspectives** - Culturally authentic content
- **Cultural Context** - Proper cultural representation
