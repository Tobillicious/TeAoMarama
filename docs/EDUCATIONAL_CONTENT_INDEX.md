
# Educational Content Index

This document serves as a central index for all educational teaching materials within the project. It is intended to assist all LLM agents in locating and understanding the structure of the curriculum content.

## 1. Core Curriculum Unit Plans

The primary, most structured teaching content is located in the `migration/unit_plans/` directory. This is the best place to start to understand the pedagogical approach.

- **Directory:** `/migration/unit_plans/`
- **Key Files:**
    - `Year8_StructuredLiteracy_UnitPlan.md`: The main unit plan for the Year 8 Structured Literacy program. It outlines the entire 6-week course.
    - `Year8_StructuredLiteracy_Lesson1.md`: The first detailed lesson plan, focusing on sentence expansion.

## 2. Supplementary Handouts & Resources

Additional teaching materials, including handouts for various subjects and year levels, are distributed across two main directories. These are excellent sources for specific content and examples.

- **Primary Directory:** `/public/resources/handouts/`
    - Contains numerous `.md` files for different subjects, such as `Y8_English_Contemporary_NZ_Literature.md` and `Y8_Science_Ecosystems_NZ.md`.

- **Recovered/Migrated Directory:** `/migration/recovered_resources/handouts/`
    - Contains additional handouts recovered during the migration process.

## 3. ERO Demonstration Content

The content specifically prepared for the ERO (Education Review Office) demonstration is referenced across multiple documents. The core teaching materials for this are the Year 8 Structured Literacy components.

- **Primary Components:**
    - **Year 8 Writing Revolution:** Based on "The Writing Revolution" methodology. Content is in `migration/unit_plans/`.
    - **Year 8 Reading Strategies:** Comprehension strategies using culturally relevant texts.
    - **Year 8 Academic Vocabulary:** Vocabulary development with a cultural context.

## 4. Raw & Migrated Content

For broader context and historical information, the following directories are useful:

- **Real Resources:** `/real-resources/`
    - Contains a variety of educational materials across different year levels.
- **Migrated Content:** `/migrated-content/`
    - Contains content that has been migrated from other systems.

This index should be updated as new educational content is created or discovered.
