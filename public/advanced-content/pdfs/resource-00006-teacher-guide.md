# Year 7 Social Studies: Resource 6 - Teacher Guide

## Lesson Overview
- **Subject:** Social Studies
- **Year Level:** Year 7
- **Duration:** 50 minutes
- **Cultural Elements:** 4

## Learning Objectives
- Understand key concepts in Social Studies
- Apply learning to real-world contexts
- Develop critical thinking skills
- Connect learning to Te Ao Māori perspectives

## Cultural Integration
This lesson integrates Te Reo Māori vocabulary and concepts, Māori worldviews and perspectives to provide authentic cultural learning experiences.

## Lesson Activities
### Introduction and Context Setting (10 minutes)
Introduce the lesson topic and connect to prior learning
**Cultural Elements:** Cultural greeting, Respectful protocols

### Main Learning Activity (25 minutes)
Core learning activity with hands-on engagement
**Cultural Elements:** Māori perspectives, Cultural connections

### Group Discussion and Reflection (10 minutes)
Students discuss learning and make connections


### Assessment and Wrap-up (5 minutes)
Quick assessment and lesson conclusion


## Assessment
**Type:** Formative Assessment
**Criteria:** Understanding of key concepts, Application of learning, Critical thinking demonstration, Cultural awareness and respect
**Rubric:** Students will be assessed on their understanding, application, and cultural awareness through observation, discussion, and written responses.
