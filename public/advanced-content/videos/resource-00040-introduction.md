# Year 7 Social Studies: Resource 40 - Introduction Video Script

## Video Overview
- **Duration:** 3-5 minutes
- **Target Audience:** Year 7 students
- **Purpose:** Introduce lesson topic and engage students

## Video Script

### Opening (30 seconds)
"Kia ora! Welcome to today's lesson on Social Studies. I'm excited to explore Year 7 Social Studies: Resource 40 with you today. This lesson will help us understand how Social Studies connects to our world and our communities."

### Learning Objectives (1 minute)
"By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:
- Understand key concepts in Social Studies
- Apply learning to real-world contexts
- Develop critical thinking skills
- Connect learning to Te Ao Māori perspectives"

### Cultural Context (1 minute)
"Today we'll be learning about Social Studies while honoring different cultural perspectives. We'll explore:
This lesson integrates Te Reo Māori vocabulary and concepts, Māori worldviews and perspectives, Cultural protocols and respect to provide authentic cultural learning experiences."

### Lesson Preview (1 minute)
"Here's what we'll be doing today:
- Introduction and Context Setting: Introduce the lesson topic and connect to prior learning...
- Main Learning Activity: Core learning activity with hands-on engagement...
- Group Discussion and Reflection: Students discuss learning and make connections...
- Assessment and Wrap-up: Quick assessment and lesson conclusion..."

### Engagement Question (30 seconds)
"Before we begin, think about this: How does Social Studies connect to your own experiences and community? We'll explore this together throughout the lesson."

### Closing (30 seconds)
"Get ready for an engaging and culturally rich learning experience. Let's begin our journey into Year 7 Social Studies: Resource 40!"

## Visual Elements
- Clear, engaging graphics
- Cultural imagery and symbols
- Student-friendly animations
- Accessibility features (subtitles, audio descriptions)

## Technical Requirements
- HD quality video
- Clear audio with background music
- Closed captions in English and Te Reo Māori
- Accessible for students with hearing impairments

## Cultural Considerations
- Appropriate cultural imagery
- Respectful representation of cultural elements
- Inclusive language and perspectives
- Cultural safety protocols followed
