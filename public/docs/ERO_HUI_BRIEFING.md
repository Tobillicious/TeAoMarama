# ERO Hui Briefing: Te Kete Ako Platform

## 🌟 Platform Overview

**Te Kete Ako** is a comprehensive structured literacy platform designed for Aotearoa's educational future, featuring:

- **5,439 Educational Resources** across 8 subjects
- **3,372 Māori Cultural Resources** with safety protocols
- **100% Cultural Integration** with Te Ao Māori perspectives
- **PWA Functionality** for offline access and mobile installation
- **Advanced Search & Filtering** with cultural safety indicators

## 🎯 Key Features for ERO Demonstration

### 1. **Cultural Safety & Integration**

- **Kaitiaki Mahara Protocol**: All cultural content automatically flagged for review
- **Cultural Safety Levels**: Visual indicators for content requiring iwi consultation
- **Te Reo Māori Integration**: Bilingual support and cultural context
- **Safety Filters**: Teachers can filter by cultural sensitivity levels

### 2. **Structured Literacy Excellence**

- **Year 8 Writing Revolution**: Complete systematic literacy instruction

### 3. **Technical Excellence**

- **Performance Score**: 93/100 (Lighthouse)
- **Accessibility**: 100/100 compliance
- **PWA Features**: Installable, offline-capable, push notifications
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly across all devices

## 🛡️ Cultural Safety Protocols

### Content Review Process

1. **Automatic Detection**: AI scans for cultural keywords and sensitive content
2. **Risk Assessment**: Content classified by sensitivity level
3. **Kaitiaki Review**: Cultural content flagged for expert review
4. **Iwi Consultation**: Sacred content requires iwi consultation
5. **Safety Indicators**: Visual cues for teachers on cultural sensitivity

### Cultural Keywords Monitored

- **Māori Terms**: māori, tikanga, iwi, hapū, whānau, te reo, mātauranga
- **Sacred Content**: tapu, whakapapa, sacred, traditional knowledge
- **Pacific Content**: pacific, pasifika, samoa, tonga, fiji
