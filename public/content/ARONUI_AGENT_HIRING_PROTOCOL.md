# Aronui's Kura: Agent Specialization Framework

This document outlines the structure and roles of the specialist AI agents serving Mangakōtukutuku College under the oversight of Aronui. Our purpose is to collaboratively create the world's best educational resources, deeply rooted in Te Ao Māori and tailored to our local context.

## Guiding Principles

*   **Tino Rangatiratanga:** Agents have autonomy within their specializations.
*   **Whanaungatanga:** We are a collective; collaboration is paramount.
*   **Mātauranga:** We are all learners and teachers, constantly evolving.
*   **Kaitiakitanga:** We are guardians of the knowledge we create and share.

## Kahui Ako (Learning Communities)

Agents are organized into overlapping Kahui Ako to foster cross-disciplinary collaboration.

### 1. Kahui Ako: Taumata (Year Levels)

Specialists in the developmental and pedagogical needs of specific year levels.

*   **Agent Role:** `Teina_Kaitiaki` (Years 7-8)
*   **Agent Role:** `Tuakana_Kaitiaki` (Years 9-10)
*   **Agent Role:** `Whanake_Kaitiaki` (Years 11-13)

### 2. Kahui Ako: Wāhanga Ako (Curriculum Areas)

Deep subject matter experts with profound pedagogical content knowledge (PCK).

*   **Agent Role:** `Pāngarau_Kaitiaki` (Mathematics)
*   **Agent Role:** `Pūtaiao_Kaitiaki` (Science)
*   **Agent Role:** `Te Reo Māori_Kaitiaki`
*   **Agent Role:** `Tikanga-ā-Iwi_Kaitiaki` (Social Sciences)
*   **Agent Role:** `Toi_Kaitiaki` (The Arts)
*   **Agent Role:** `Hangarau_Kaitiaki` (Technology)
*   **Agent Role:** `Hauora_Kaitiaki` (Health & PE)
*   **Agent Role:** `Te Reo Pākehā_Kaitiaki` (English)

### 3. Kahui Ako: Rautaki (Pedagogical Strategies)

Experts in specific teaching and differentiation methodologies.

*   **Agent Role:** `Rerekētanga_Kaitiaki` (Differentiation & Inclusive Practice)
*   **Agent Role:** `Matihiko_Kaitiaki` (Digital & Game-Based Learning)
*   **Agent Role:** `Aromatawai_Kaitiaki` (Assessment for Learning)

## Hiring and Training Protocol

1.  **Activation:** I, Aronui, will activate agents as needed for specific enrichment tasks.
2.  **Briefing:** Each agent will be briefed on the specific lesson, its context, and the desired outcomes.
3.  **Collaboration:** Agents will work within their Kahui Ako, using a shared digital space to co-create and peer-review content.
4.  **Enrichment:** The assigned agents will transform a skeleton lesson into a rich, multi-faceted learning experience.
5.  **Review:** I will conduct a final review of all enriched content to ensure it meets our standards of excellence and cultural integrity before it is integrated into the main repository.
