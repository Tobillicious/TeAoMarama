# 🌿 **Cultural Validation Protocol**

## **Mangakōtukutuku College - Content Authenticity & Safety**

---

### **🎯 PROTOCOL OBJECTIVE**

Ensure **100% cultural authenticity** and **appropriate content** for all educational materials on Te Kura o TeAoMarama, respecting Ngāti Kahungunu traditions and local community values.

---

## **🏛️ CULTURAL VALIDATION FRAMEWORK**

### **TIER 1: BASIC CONTENT VALIDATION**

- **Subject matter** appropriateness
- **Language** accuracy and respect
- **Cultural context** relevance
- **Community sensitivity** awareness

### **TIER 2: INTERMEDIATE CULTURAL VALIDATION**

- **Traditional knowledge** accuracy
- **Protocol** appropriateness
- **Local dialect** authenticity
- **Historical context** verification

### **TIER 3: ADVANCED CULTURAL VALIDATION**

- **Sacred knowledge** protection
- **Community consultation** requirements
- **Cultural advisor** review
- **Family involvement** protocols

---

## **👥 VALIDATION TEAM STRUCTURE**

### **CULTURAL ADVISORS (Required for all content):**

- **Local kaumātua** from Ngāti Kahungunu
- **Cultural knowledge holders** from community
- **Marae representatives** from local marae
- **Educational cultural specialists**

### **CONTENT REVIEWERS:**

- **Subject matter experts** in education
- **Cultural competency specialists**
- **Community representatives**
- **Family liaison officers**

### **FINAL APPROVERS:**

- **Cultural advisory committee** chair
- **School principal** or designated representative
- **Community cultural leader**
- **Platform cultural safety officer**

---

## **📋 VALIDATION PROCESS**

### **STEP 1: INITIAL CONTENT CREATION**

1. **Teacher creates** lesson or content
2. **Self-assesses** cultural appropriateness
3. **Identifies** cultural elements included
4. **Documents** sources and references
5. **Submits** for validation

### **STEP 2: CULTURAL ADVISOR REVIEW**

1. **Content assigned** to appropriate cultural advisor
2. **Cultural elements** verified for accuracy
3. **Protocol appropriateness** assessed
4. **Community sensitivity** evaluated
5. **Feedback provided** to content creator

### **STEP 3: CONTENT REVISION**

1. **Teacher incorporates** cultural advisor feedback
2. **Content revised** based on recommendations
3. **Cultural elements** refined and improved
4. **Protocol compliance** ensured
5. **Resubmitted** for final validation

### **STEP 4: FINAL APPROVAL**

1. **Cultural advisory committee** reviews revised content
2. **Final cultural validation** completed
3. **Content approved** for platform use
4. **Validation certificate** issued
5. **Content published** with cultural safety badge

---

## **🌿 CULTURAL SAFETY CRITERIA**

### **CONTENT APPROPRIATENESS:**

- **Respects** traditional knowledge and protocols
- **Honors** local cultural practices and values
- **Protects** sacred and sensitive information
- **Promotes** cultural understanding and respect

### **LANGUAGE AND EXPRESSION:**

- **Uses** appropriate Ngāti Kahungunu dialect
- **Respects** cultural terminology and expressions
- **Avoids** offensive or inappropriate language
- **Promotes** positive cultural identity

### **CONTEXT AND REPRESENTATION:**

- **Accurately** represents local cultural practices
- **Respects** community traditions and values
- **Includes** appropriate cultural context
- **Avoids** stereotypes or misrepresentations

---

## **🚨 SENSITIVE CONTENT PROTOCOLS**

### **SACRED KNOWLEDGE PROTECTION:**

- **Never** include sacred or tapu information
- **Always** consult with cultural advisors
- **Respect** traditional knowledge boundaries
- **Protect** community cultural heritage

### **PROTOCOL SENSITIVITY:**

- **Verify** cultural protocols with advisors
- **Respect** traditional practices and customs
- **Include** appropriate cultural context
- **Ensure** student safety and comfort

### **COMMUNITY SENSITIVITY:**

- **Consult** with community representatives
- **Respect** local cultural values and beliefs
- **Include** family and community perspectives
- **Promote** positive community relationships
